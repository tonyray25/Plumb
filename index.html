The Problem

The fine print is where profits go to die.

In DFW's booming construction market, general contractors increasingly shift risk downstream through complex contract language. One missed clause can cost you the entire job.

⚠

Hidden Liquidated Damages

Punitive delay penalties buried in general conditions that can exceed $2,500/day—with no cap.

◈

Toxic Indemnification

Clauses designed to bypass the Texas Anti-Indemnity Act, exposing you to unlimited liability.

◇

Scope Gaps & Overlaps

HVAC starters in electrical specs, fire stops in mechanical—hidden costs that destroy margins.

⬡

Pay-If-Paid Traps

Cash flow killers that force you to finance projects for months without recourse.

◎

Extended Warranties

Two-year warranty demands when your suppliers only cover one—you're on the hook for the difference.

⬢

Retainage Abuse

10% holdbacks with vague release conditions that turn into permanent deductions.
